References & influences

Standing on good shoulders.

My approach didn't appear from nowhere. It's built on decades of thinking by people who shaped how we model, historize and govern data — and on collaborators I've built real things with. I've written about many of them — here's where to find their work.

Collaboration

CrossBreeze — building Model-Driven Data Engineering together.

I've worked closely with CrossBreeze and its founders, Harmen Wessels and Willem Otten, on Model-Driven Data Engineering. I first met them on the Rabobank project, and later invited them to help build the MDDE framework at de Volksbank / ASN.

Their CrossModel tooling — open-source, logical-model-first, generating schemas and transformations from metadata — is MDDE in product form. A genuine meeting of minds on the same vision: structure first, generate the rest.
