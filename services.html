On rates

I price engagements, not days.

A day-rate turns senior work into a line item. I'd rather understand what you're trying to achieve and propose an engagement that fits the value — fixed scope where it makes sense, a retainer where it doesn't.

The honest first step is a conversation. If it's a fit, you'll get a clear proposal. If it isn't, I'll tell you that too.
